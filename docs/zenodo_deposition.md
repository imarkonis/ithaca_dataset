## Purpose

This Zenodo record preserves two supporting input datasets used in the
ITHACA terrestrial water-cycle analysis workflow:

- annual GPCC v2022 precipitation for 1981–2020;
- annual MERRA-2/MSWX potential evapotranspiration for 1980–2024.
